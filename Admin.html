How Our Scraper Works

Our automated scores scraper simplifies the process of tracking and evaluating your performance on coding platforms. Here's how it works:

Upload an Excel sheet containing user data with specific columns for Codeforces, GFG, LeetCode, CodeChef, and optional HackerRank information.

The scraper processes the data, calculates scores, and generates a detailed Excel sheet with ratings and scores across the mentioned platforms.

Download the generated Excel sheet to explore your individual and overall performance.

Experience the convenience of tracking your progress seamlessly with our automated scraper!
